SOS

In your SPOT/InReach account, update your Primary Emergency Contact to be the competition Safety Director:

    Name: ${SafetyDirector}
    Phone: ${SafetyDirectorPhone} (${SafetyDirectorPhoneProvider})

Set your Secondary Emergency Contact to be someone traveling with you, or a personal/family contact.

Update your Emergency information (under "additional information" for SPOT and "Emergency Notes" for InReach) to include the following message. This will allow meet personnel to obtain your information from InReach/SPOT in the event that you have pressed the SOS button. Again, please copy and paste the following section into your device configuration:

${PilotInfo}, is a participant in a paragliding competition in ${Location}. The Safety Director ${SafetyDirector} can be contacted at ${SafetyDirectorPhone}. The Meet Organizer ${MeetOrganizer} can be contacted at ${MeetOrganizerPhone}. <Include known medical conditions / info>

Be sure to add any other information that emergency services might need: allergies, special medical instructions, etc.
